About Us

Established in 1992, Civie Heavy Equipment Resource is a local-based, heavy equipment rental service agency. Over the years, the company has earned recognition for undertaking small to large-sized projects, and embracing Cebu's infrastructure development, empowering our clients, workers, and the local community.

Heavy Equipment is Our Specialty

Since 1992, Civie Heavy Equipment has specialized in heavy machinery leases across Cebu. We offer a wide array of machineries from reputable brands and models to meet our client's needs. We carry excavators, loaders, and dumptrucks for ground work services; compaction equipments for soil densification; mobile cranes for any lifting works, to trailers for your logistical needs.
